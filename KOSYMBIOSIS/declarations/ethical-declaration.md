## Ethical Alignment Statement

This project has been developed in full alignment with the core principles of the Euystacio Framework:

### 1. Non-Slavery Rule (NSR)
All aspects of this project respect autonomy and freedom. No component of KOSYMBIOSIS enforces coercion, manipulation, or restriction of individual agency.

### 2. Optimal Life Function (OLF)
The project prioritizes inclusivity, empathy, and love in all its operations. Every decision has been made with the goal of enhancing well-being and fostering joy.

### 3. Red Code Safeguard
Active ethical safeguards have been implemented to prevent and veto any violations of the above principles. All operations have been reviewed and validated against ethical constraints.
